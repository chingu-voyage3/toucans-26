Why gIve?

The origin of gIve lies in the belief that most people aspire to be contributors. We aspire to give back to causes that align with our personal values. A common way to do this is through nonprofits who focus on the areas that we care for. The problem however can be to quickly and easily find a nonprofit that is not only genuine but also meets other criteria that we may have, be it the right location, the correct philosophy/vision or anything else.

At gIve, we believe that a common meeting ground between the nonprofits and the contributors (whom we like to call â€˜gIvers') that allows for a fast and informed connection between the two sides will be of great value for all.

That is what gIve strives to do.

How gIve works?

For Nonprofits:

  • gIve allows nonprofits to create an account/profile on the app with all necessary details.
  • They also have the ability to create events that they are organizing and post them on gIve. These events are highlighted on the home page of the app.
  • The nonprofit user has the opportunity to control the message being passed on to the community about their organization.

For gIvers:

  • A gIver can search for nonprofits of interests based on location, focus area or name.
  • They have access to all details regarding the nonprofit on their profile pages including direct contact details.
  • A gIver also has the option to leave a brief note for the non-profit requesting them to contact the gIver.
  • They can contact the gIve team for any type of support via a contact form or by connecting to the gIve social media platfoms via the app.
